Rotating the camera

After we created the peptide we can freely position the camera: To do so click with a mouse button into the 3D graphics view and drag the mouse:

   Left button: Rotate
   Middle or Wheel: Zoom
   Right button: Move view

When you are finished playing around with the camera, center the camera on the peptide: Find the widget with the name Structures and highlight the System by clicking on its name ("ARND").
Then press the right mouse button to open a context menu and press on the Focus entry.
This will enable the next step of the tutorial.
Press Next to continue.
