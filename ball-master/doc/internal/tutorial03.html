Hierarchy of molecules

Now lets have a look at the peptides hierarchy.
The "Structures" widget contains a hierarchical list of all loaded molecules. Click the cross to the left of the name of your newly created protein to open the hierarchy tree.
Repeat this for the appearing levels to see, that the peptide clearly consists of four amino acids (if you entered the sequence correctly).

The checkboxes at the right side of the entries can be used to ''mark'' entries e.g. for identifying them in the 3D view. Try to check on some box and then uncheck it again.

When you are finished playing around with the hierarchy, ''highlight'' the System by clicking on its name ("ARND").
This will take you to the next step of the tutorial.
Press Next to continue.
